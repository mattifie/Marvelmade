The Recyclery

ABOUT THE RECYCLERY

The Recyclery brings unsuspecting bike mechanics, environmental enthusiasts, and volunteers together in completing an unlikely task: restoring old and discarded bicycles. The program focuses on drawing students from area high schools to comprise diverse groups of bike-fixing volunteers. In these groups, students learn practical bike mechanics and creative problem solving from a local bike expert.

This year so far, the Recyclery has attracted over 65 new high school volunteers, which signifies rapid growth for the recently established program. Rapid growth not only means more progam participants, but also calls for more volunteers and donors! This is where you can make significant contributions, either in time or monetary donations.

Jared, a senior in high school and enthusiastic program participant, fervently states that The Recyclery has not only piqued his interest in bikes, but also in making healthy, productive use of his time in general. "I used to think after school sports were the only option for me. I like participating in athletics, but the Recyclery combines this weird mix of nerdy and creative that I never thought I'd like, but now I know I love."
