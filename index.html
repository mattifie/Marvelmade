THE RECYCLERY

>>>>>>> master

The Recyclery brings unsuspecting bike mechanics, environmental enthusiasts, and volunteers together in completing an unlikely task: restoring old and discarded bicycles. The program focuses on drawing students from area high schools to comprise diverse groups of bike-fixing volunteers. In these groups, students learn practical bike mechanics and creative problem solving from a local bike expert.

Furthermore, volunteer groups often become tight-knit social groups that continue to foster the intellectual & creative environment The Recyclery strives to build.

POPULAR CAMPAIGNS


Photo Finish

Anyone who thinks they might like photography is bound to flock to Photo Finish. Geared toward those with even a fleeting interest in the medium, Photo Finish teaches participants the art & importance of journalistic photography.

Afterschool Exchange

Did you ever wonder what school was like in another country? If so, you'll find answers to your questions with the Afterschool Exchange program. Participants share skype sessions and emails with a partner school in another part of the world.

Improv(e)

Kids are inherently creative. Improv(e) engages gradeschool aged children in the art of improvisational acting. Spur of the moment plays and comic sketches are best written and acted by kids – we promise. Improv(e) = self-expression and confidence.
